Get Licensed — In Order

Each step unlocks the next. ~2–3 weeks total. ~$275–$625 in fees.
1
Form Your LLC
$100–$200 · geauxbiz.sos.la.gov · 1–3 days · Do this first — every other application requires your LLC name and EIN
1b
Get Your EIN (Federal Tax ID)
Free · IRS.gov · Instant · Required to open a business bank account
1c
Open a Business Bank Account
Free–$15/mo · Any bank · Same day · Keep business and personal finances separate from day one
2
State Sales Tax Permit
Free · revenue.louisiana.gov · 1–3 days · Required before legally collecting sales tax
3
Louisiana Vending Machine Operator Permit
$75/year · revenue.louisiana.gov · 1–5 days · Must be physically affixed to front of machine
4
EBR Parish Occupational License
$50–$200 · brgov.com · 1–5 days · Some bar owners will ask to see this before signing
5
Health Permit (Food Products)
$50–$150 · LDH / EBR Health Unit · Varies · Required for snacks, Liquid IV, energy shots
6
Vape Age Verification Compliance
Hardware cost (built into machine) · Louisiana law requires ID scanner for all vape sales · No exceptions

Pitch Bar Owners

Visit Mon–Wed afternoon. Independent owners only at first.
"I place a fully stocked vending machine in your bar at zero cost to you. You get 15% of all revenue — or $75/month flat. I handle all stocking, maintenance, and repairs. Your customers stay in your bar instead of leaving to find a vape or snack."
Start with 15% Commission
Lower friction to get the first yes. Switch to flat fee once revenue is steady.
Switch to $75/Month Flat
Once you're established, flat fee gives you better margins as revenue grows.
Independent Bars First
One conversation = one decision. Chain managers require corporate approval.
Get a Signed Agreement
Include: revenue %, machine ownership, 30-day exit clause, electricity access.

6-Week Launch Checklist

From LLC to first sale in 6 weeks. Progress saves automatically.
0 / 0 done
Week 1 — Legal & Business Setup
Register LLC at geauxBIZ portal ($100–$200)
Get EIN at IRS.gov (free — instant)
Open a business bank account
Apply for Louisiana State Sales Tax Permit (free)
Apply for Louisiana Vending Machine Operator Permit ($75)
Apply for EBR Parish Occupational License ($50–$200)
Week 2 — Find Your Machine
Set budget — aim $3,500–$5,500 for AMS 39-640 refurb
Search MegaVending.com, UsedVending.com, Vending.com, eBay/Craigslist
Inspect before buying — test card reader, coils, refrigeration
Confirm cashless payment included or budget $150–$300 add-on
Order condoms for machine — Regular, Large, and Flavored (Sam's Club or Costco)
Week 3 — Scout & Pitch Locations
Visit Third Street / Tigerland Thu or Fri night — count crowds, check layouts
Build list of 5–10 target bars (independents first)
Pitch bar owners Mon–Wed afternoon
Get signed location agreement (revenue %, ownership, 30-day exit, electricity)
Week 4 — Prep Your Machine
Purchase machine after location is locked
Clean and test all coils and payment system
Install cashless payment reader if needed (Nayax, Cantaloupe, or Parlevel)
Create product labels for every slot
Affix Louisiana vending permit to front of machine
Week 5 — Source & Stock Products
Order vapes from licensed wholesale distributor
Order condoms — Sam's Club or Costco
Order Liquid IV / energy shots — Costco or Amazon Business
Order snacks — Sam's Club or local wholesale
Order CBD/drops — direct from brand or specialty distributor
Price each product ($2–$5 above retail — convenience premium)
Fill every slot and photograph for inventory tracking
Week 6 — Install & Launch
Deliver and install machine at bar
Stock condom slots at eye level in the machine — Regular, Large, Flavored
Run test transaction on every payment method
Brief bar staff — what's in it and who to call for jams
Take baseline inventory photos
Ongoing — Weekly / Bi-Weekly
Restock every 5–7 days minimum
Never leave a slot empty going into a weekend
Log what sells — cut slow movers after 30 days
Track revenue vs. expenses in a spreadsheet
Pay bar owner commission on schedule
Wipe glass, check for jams, test card reader monthly
Month 3 — Review & Decide
Calculate actual monthly net profit
Identify top 5 products — expand their slot count
Renegotiate to flat fee if revenue is stable
If profitable → scout Machine #2 location
If underperforming → diagnose: location? products? machine?

Growth Roadmap

Scale after Machine 1 is profitable and understood.
Phase 1 · Months 1–3
Prove the Concept
1 machine live. Learn restocking rhythm, track best sellers, build bar owner relationship.
Phase 2 · Months 4–6
Add Machine #2
Reinvest profits. Place second machine in a new bar — different area from Machine 1.
Phase 3 · Months 7–12
Add Vape Kiosk + Expand Areas
Add a dedicated vape kiosk at top-performing location. Expand to Mid City and Perkins corridor.
Phase 4 · Year 2+
Scale to 6–10 Machines
Hire part-time restock driver. Expand to Gonzales, Denham Springs, Hammond. Consider machine branding wraps.
